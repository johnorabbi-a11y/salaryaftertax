How is weekly net pay worked out?

The weekly figure is the estimated annual take-home pay divided by 52. Actual payslips can vary slightly depending on bonus timing, payroll setup and workplace deductions.

For a salary at this level, much of the income sits in the additional rate band, so weekly deductions are substantial.
