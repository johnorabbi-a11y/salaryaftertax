Why high salaries pay more tax

Higher incomes are taxed across multiple bands in the UK system. Once income exceeds £125,140 the Personal Allowance disappears and most additional income is taxed at higher or additional rates.

This means the gap between gross pay and net monthly salary increases significantly for higher earners.
