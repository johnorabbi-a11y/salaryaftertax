How Income Tax Works in the USA

The United States uses a progressive federal tax system. Workers also pay payroll taxes for Social Security and Medicare.

State taxes vary widely — some states like Texas and Florida have no state income tax, while others such as California and New York have higher rates.

What Can Change Your Take-Home Pay?

  • Your state income tax
  • Filing status (single, married)
  • Health insurance premiums
  • 401(k) retirement contributions
  • Tax credits or deductions
