Assumptions Used

  • Single filer using the standard deduction
  • No state income tax included
  • No retirement or benefits deductions included
  • No credits, itemized deductions, or bonus-specific adjustments included
  • Figures shown as rounded estimates

What Affects Monthly Take-Home Pay?

  • State taxes: these can materially lower monthly net income.
  • Pre-tax deductions: retirement and healthcare deductions reduce taxable wages.
  • Variable pay: bonus and stock compensation can change actual payroll patterns.
  • Filing status: household tax setup affects withholding and final tax.
  • Payroll method: semimonthly and biweekly payroll can make real deposits differ from simple monthly estimates.
