£120,000 Take Home Pay in the UK

A salary of £120,000 per year is considered a high income in the UK. At this level, the personal allowance is largely removed and the higher tax bands apply.

Why Net Pay Drops at Higher Salaries

At higher income levels in the UK, multiple factors reduce the proportion of take-home pay:

  • Higher rate tax (40%)
  • Loss of personal allowance above £100k
  • Additional tax bands above £125k
