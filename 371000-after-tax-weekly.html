Short tax explanation

For very high salaries, the bulk of additional earnings is taxed at the additional rate. National Insurance still applies, although at the reduced upper percentage above the upper earnings limit. This is why take-home pay rises steadily, but not in line with the full gross increase.

What can change your weekly take-home pay?

  • Tax code corrections or underpayments
  • Bonus or commission payments
  • Workplace pension deductions
  • Student loan repayments
  • Other payroll deductions or taxable benefits
