What affects take-home pay on $500,000?

The biggest factor beyond this estimate is location. State and city taxes can materially reduce net pay at this income level. Filing jointly instead of single may also change the effective federal burden. Pre-tax deductions, executive benefits, and deferred compensation structures can shift both current take-home and longer-term tax outcomes. This page gives you a solid base case to compare against your actual pay arrangement and nearby salary levels.
