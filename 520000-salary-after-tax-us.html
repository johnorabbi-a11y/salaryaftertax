What affects take-home pay on $520,000?

The biggest missing variable from this estimate is state income tax. At a $520,000 salary, the difference between a no-tax state and a high-tax state can be very large over the course of a year. Filing status, retirement contributions, health insurance costs, and executive compensation structure also matter. If part of your pay comes through bonuses, stock compensation, or deferred pay, actual withholding may look much harsher in specific pay periods than this smooth annualized estimate suggests.
