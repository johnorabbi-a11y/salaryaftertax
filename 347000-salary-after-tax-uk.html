Why is tax so high at £347,000?

At this salary level, your Personal Allowance is fully removed, so all of your taxable income is charged through the higher and additional rate bands. Most of your earnings above the additional rate threshold are taxed at 45%, while employee National Insurance still applies on qualifying earnings.

What can change your take-home pay?

  • Pension salary sacrifice contributions
  • Bonus payments or irregular income
  • Student loan repayments
  • Company benefits and taxable perks
  • A different tax code
