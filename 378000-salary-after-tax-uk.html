Short tax explanation

At very high salary levels in the UK, the personal allowance has already been completely removed. Most additional earnings fall into the additional-rate tax band, meaning each pay rise increases take-home pay but a large portion goes to tax.

What can change your take-home pay?

  • Pension contributions
  • Student loan repayments
  • Salary sacrifice schemes
  • Bonuses or commission payments
  • Changes to your HMRC tax code
