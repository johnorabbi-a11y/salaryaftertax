£22 an Hour Salary (UK)

If you earn £22 per hour, your yearly salary depends on how many hours you work and how many weeks you are paid for.

Quick salary conversions

40 hours per week

  • Weekly: £880
  • Monthly: £3,813
  • Annual: £45,760

Assumes 52 paid weeks. Monthly = annual ÷ 12.

37.5 hours per week

  • Monthly: £3,575
  • Annual: £42,900

35 hours per week

  • Weekly: £770
  • Monthly: £3,337
  • Annual: £40,040

What about take-home pay?

Your take-home pay depends on your tax code, pension contributions, student loan and other deductions.

  • Monthly pay = annual salary ÷ 12
