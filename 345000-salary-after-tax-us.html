What can change take-home pay?

  • State income tax: this estimate assumes none, so take-home pay will usually be lower in states that tax wages.
  • Pre-tax deductions: 401(k) contributions, HSA contributions, and health premiums can reduce taxable income and change your net pay.
  • Filing situation: this page uses a single filer with the standard deduction only.
  • Bonuses or mixed compensation: supplemental wages can be withheld differently during the year.
  • Additional payroll adjustments: real paychecks can differ slightly depending on payroll setup and withholding elections.
