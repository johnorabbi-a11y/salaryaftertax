What affects weekly take-home pay?

At $510,000 a year, weekly take-home can shift sharply depending on where you live and how your compensation is structured. State income tax may be the single biggest missing factor in this estimate. Bonus-heavy compensation, stock vesting, deferred pay, and executive benefit packages can also make real weekly pay much lumpier than a smooth annualized model suggests. That is why this page works best as a consistent comparison point across nearby salary levels.
