Why deductions rise at higher salaries

Once your income reaches this level, all earnings are taxable and a substantial share falls into the highest tax band. That means marginal earnings are heavily taxed, which is why take-home pay rises more slowly than gross salary.

What can change your take-home pay?

  • Pension and salary sacrifice contributions
  • Student loan repayments
  • Bonus and commission payments
  • Benefits in kind
  • Different tax code settings
