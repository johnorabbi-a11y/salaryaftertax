Why Weekly Pay Matters

Weekly income is useful for comparing job offers, understanding cashflow, and evaluating how much you truly earn relative to effort and time.

Even with a very high salary, the tax system significantly reduces your real income — which is why weekly net pay gives a clearer perspective.
