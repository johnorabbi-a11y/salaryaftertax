Why take-home pay rises more slowly than salary

Once you are firmly into the additional rate band and your Personal Allowance is gone, each extra increase in gross pay adds less to your net income. That is why higher salaries still improve take-home pay, but the increase looks modest compared with the extra gross amount earned.

What can change your take-home pay?

  • Pension salary sacrifice contributions
  • Student loan repayments
  • Bonus and commission income
  • Company benefits and taxable perks
  • Tax code changes during the year
