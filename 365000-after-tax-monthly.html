Why is monthly take-home lower than gross pay?

Once earnings move into the higher and additional rate bands, a larger share of income is taxed at 40% and 45%. On a £365,000 salary, the personal allowance is also fully removed.

That makes the difference between gross monthly pay and net monthly pay much wider than it is at lower salary levels.

What can change your monthly net pay?

  • Salary sacrifice pension arrangements
  • Student loan deductions
  • Benefits in kind
  • Bonus timing through payroll
  • Scottish tax status
