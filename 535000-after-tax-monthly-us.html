Assumptions Used

  • Single filer using the standard deduction
  • No state or local income tax included
  • No retirement contributions or benefit deductions included
  • No itemized deductions, credits, RSUs, or bonus-specific adjustments included
  • Figures shown as rounded planning estimates rather than payroll advice

What Affects Monthly Take-Home Pay?

  • State taxes: these can materially reduce net monthly income.
  • Pre-tax deductions: 401(k), health insurance, and HSA contributions lower taxable wages.
  • Compensation structure: salary, bonus, and stock compensation can produce different withholding patterns.
  • Filing status: joint filing or dependents can shift federal outcomes.
  • Payroll timing: actual monthly deposits can vary if pay is biweekly or bonus-heavy.
