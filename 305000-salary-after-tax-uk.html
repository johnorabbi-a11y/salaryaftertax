How tax works on a £305,000 salary

At this level of income the Personal Allowance is completely withdrawn. The majority of earnings fall into the higher and additional rate tax bands, meaning a large share of income is taxed at 40% and 45% rates.

National Insurance also applies to earnings through PAYE, though the rate drops once income passes the upper earnings threshold.

Things that could affect your take-home pay

Your real net pay may vary if you make pension contributions, salary sacrifice arrangements, student loan repayments, childcare deductions, bonuses, or receive taxable benefits through your employer.
