£29 an Hour Salary UK

If you earn £29 per hour and work full-time in the UK, your estimated annual salary is around £60,320 per year.

£29 an Hour Pay Breakdown

Pay period Gross Pay
Yearly £60,320
Monthly £5,027
Weekly £1,160

How is this calculated?

The calculation assumes a standard full-time schedule of 40 hours per week and 52 weeks per year.

£29 × 40 hours × 52 weeks = £60,320 yearly salary.

Is £29 an hour a good wage in the UK?

£29 per hour is a strong hourly wage in the UK and well above the national average hourly pay. It is common in professional roles, contracting work and experienced skilled trades.
