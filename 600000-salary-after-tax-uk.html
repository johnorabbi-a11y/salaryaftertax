£600,000 Salary After Tax UK

Why is tax high on £600,000?

At this income level your Personal Allowance has been completely removed and almost all income is taxed at the additional rate.

What could change your take-home pay?

Your actual take-home pay may vary depending on pension contributions, bonuses, student loan repayments or salary sacrifice schemes.
