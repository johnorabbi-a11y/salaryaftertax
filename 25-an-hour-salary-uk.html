£25 an Hour Salary (UK)

If you earn £25 per hour, your yearly salary depends on how many hours you work and how many weeks you are paid for. Below are common UK scenarios.

Quick salary conversions

40 hours per week

  • Weekly: £1,000
  • Monthly: £4,333
  • Annual: £52,000

Assumes 52 paid weeks. Monthly = annual ÷ 12.

37.5 hours per week

  • Weekly: £937.50
  • Monthly: £4,062.50
  • Annual: £48,750

35 hours per week

  • Weekly: £875
  • Monthly: £3,791.67
  • Annual: £45,500

What about take-home pay?

Your take-home pay depends on your tax code, pension contributions, student loan and any other deductions. For a proper estimate, use the calculator below and choose UK.

How the calculation works

  • Weekly pay = hourly rate × hours worked
  • Annual pay = weekly pay × paid weeks per year
  • Monthly pay = annual salary ÷ 12
