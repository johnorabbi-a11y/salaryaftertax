Why is tax so high at £348,000?

At this income level, your Personal Allowance is fully lost, which means your earnings are taxed without the usual tax-free amount. A large portion of your income is also taxed at the additional rate, so the total deduction figure becomes much higher than it is at mid-range salaries.

What can change your take-home pay?

  • Pension salary sacrifice contributions
  • Student loan repayments
  • Bonus structure and timing
  • Company car or other taxable benefits
  • Changes to your tax code
