What Affects Take-Home Pay?

  • State income tax: this can materially reduce net income compared with a no-tax state.
  • 401(k) contributions: pre-tax retirement deductions can reduce taxable income.
  • Benefits costs: healthcare, insurance and payroll deductions lower take-home pay.
  • Bonus and stock income: supplemental compensation may be withheld differently.
  • Filing status: married filing jointly may change the final net result.
