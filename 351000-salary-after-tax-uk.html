Why take-home pay rises slowly at this level

Most of the extra income above the additional rate threshold is taxed at 45%, so each pay rise adds less to your net income than it would at lower salary bands. High earners also lose their Personal Allowance completely.

What can change your take-home pay?

  • Pension salary sacrifice contributions
  • Student loan deductions
  • Commission and bonus payments
  • Benefits in kind from work
  • Different PAYE tax codes
