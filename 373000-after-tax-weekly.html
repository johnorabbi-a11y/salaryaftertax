Short tax explanation

For very high salaries, most extra income falls into the additional rate tax band. National Insurance still applies too, although at the reduced upper percentage above the upper earnings limit. This means take-home pay keeps rising, but less quickly than gross pay.

What can change your weekly take-home pay?

  • Tax code corrections or underpayments
  • Bonus or commission payments
  • Workplace pension deductions
  • Student loan repayments
  • Other payroll deductions or taxable benefits
