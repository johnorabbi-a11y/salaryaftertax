Assumptions Used

  • Single filer using the standard deduction
  • No state income tax included
  • No retirement, health, or other pre-tax deductions included
  • No bonus-specific withholding treatment included
  • Weekly figures are annual estimates divided across 52 weeks

What Affects Weekly Take-Home Pay?

  • Pay schedule: actual payroll may be biweekly rather than weekly.
  • State tax position: state taxes can significantly reduce real take-home pay.
  • Benefits deductions: health insurance and retirement contributions reduce net cash.
  • Variable compensation: bonus and stock-based pay can distort weekly averages.
  • Filing status: this affects federal withholding and final tax liability.
