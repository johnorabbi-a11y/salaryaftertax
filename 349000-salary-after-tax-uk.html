How tax works on £349,000

At this salary level, your Personal Allowance is fully removed and a large share of your income falls into the additional rate band. That means a high proportion of every extra pound earned is lost to tax, while employee National Insurance still applies on qualifying earnings.

What can change your take-home pay?

  • Pension salary sacrifice
  • Student loan repayments
  • Bonuses and irregular income
  • Benefits in kind
  • Tax code changes
