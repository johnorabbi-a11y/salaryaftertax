What Affects Weekly Take-Home Pay?

  • Pay frequency: many employers pay biweekly rather than weekly, so actual checks may land differently.
  • State taxes: these can significantly reduce weekly take-home pay.
  • Benefits deductions: insurance and retirement deductions lower net pay.
  • Bonus timing: variable comp can create very uneven real-world weekly income.
  • Filing status: tax withholding changes based on household tax setup.
