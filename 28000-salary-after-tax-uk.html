£28,000 Salary After Tax UK

Estimated take-home pay for a £28,000 salary in the UK.

If you earn £28,000 per year in the UK, your estimated take-home pay after Income Tax and National Insurance is roughly £22,960 per year.

That works out at approximately £1,913 per month after tax for the 2025/26 tax year.

£28,000 after tax breakdown

A £28,000 salary is taxed under the UK PAYE system. After the personal allowance, the remaining income is taxed at the basic rate along with National Insurance contributions.

  • Income tax: roughly £3,086 per year
  • National Insurance: roughly £1,954 per year
  • Total estimated deductions: about £5,040

That leaves a net annual salary of around £22,960.

£28,000 salary take-home per week

After tax, a £28,000 salary is roughly £441 per week.

Your actual take-home pay may vary depending on pension contributions, student loan repayments, overtime, bonuses, and other deductions.
