£16 an Hour Salary (UK)

If you earn £16 per hour, your yearly salary depends on how many hours you work and how many weeks you are paid.

Salary conversion example

  • Weekly: £640
  • Monthly: £2,773
  • Annual: £33,280

How this is calculated

  • £16 × 40 hours = £640 per week
  • £640 × 52 weeks = £33,280 per year
  • £33,280 ÷ 12 = £2,773 per month
