Salary After Tax in the United States

This guide explains how salary after tax works in the US and what your take-home pay may look like after federal income tax, Social Security and Medicare deductions.

The examples across AfterTaxTool assume a single filer using the standard deduction and no state income tax. Your real take-home pay can vary depending on state tax, benefits and retirement contributions.
