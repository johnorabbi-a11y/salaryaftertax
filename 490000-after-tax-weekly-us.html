What affects weekly take-home pay?

Weekly net pay can look quite different from a clean annual estimate once real-life payroll details are added. A high-tax state can reduce weekly take-home significantly. Large 401(k) or HSA contributions can pull down weekly cash pay while improving long-term tax efficiency. Executive compensation packages with bonuses or equity can make pay periods uneven, and benefit deductions often create paycheck-to-paycheck variation that an annualized estimate smooths out. That is why this page works best as a comparison tool rather than a literal payroll stub forecast.
