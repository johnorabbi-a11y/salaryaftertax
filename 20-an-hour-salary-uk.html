£20 an Hour Salary (UK)

If you earn £20 per hour, your yearly salary depends on how many hours you work and how many weeks you are paid for. Below are common full-time scenarios used in the UK.

Quick salary conversions

40 hours per week

  • Weekly: £800
  • Monthly: £3,466
  • Annual: £41,600

Assumes 52 paid weeks.

37.5 hours per week

  • Weekly: £750
  • Monthly: £3,250
  • Annual: £39,000

35 hours per week

  • Weekly: £700
  • Monthly: £3,033
  • Annual: £36,400

How the calculation works

  • Weekly pay = hourly rate × hours worked
  • Annual pay = weekly pay × weeks per year
  • Monthly pay = annual salary ÷ 12
