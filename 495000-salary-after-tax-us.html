What affects take-home pay on $495,000?

The biggest swing factor is still where you live. State and city income taxes can pull annual take-home down sharply, while no-tax states leave much more of the salary intact. Filing status, 401(k) deferrals, HSA contributions, and benefit elections also matter. If part of your compensation arrives as a bonus or equity, actual withholding can feel far more aggressive in individual pay periods than this smooth annual estimate suggests.
