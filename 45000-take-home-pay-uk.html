What is £45,000 take home pay in the UK?

A salary of £45,000 in the UK results in approximately £36,180 take-home pay after income tax and National Insurance. This works out to around £3,015 per month and about £696 per week.

Your actual net salary can vary depending on pension contributions, tax code, student loans, and any other deductions taken through payroll.
