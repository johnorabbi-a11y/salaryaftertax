How salary tax works in the UK

When you earn a salary in the UK, your employer deducts income tax and National Insurance through the PAYE system.

Income tax is charged in bands. Basic rate tax applies first, followed by higher rate tax and then the additional rate for high earners.

National Insurance is charged on earnings above the primary threshold and helps fund state benefits such as the NHS and state pension.
