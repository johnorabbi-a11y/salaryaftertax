Why the monthly take-home is lower than you might expect

At this income level, your personal allowance is fully withdrawn and most additional earnings are taxed at higher rates. This creates a much bigger difference between gross monthly salary and net monthly salary.

Although National Insurance rates reduce on earnings above the main threshold, Income Tax still makes up the biggest part of the deduction.

What can affect monthly net pay?

  • Salary sacrifice pension arrangements can reduce taxable pay.
  • Bonuses paid in a specific month can temporarily increase deductions.
  • Student loans, benefits in kind, and tax code changes can all alter net monthly income.
  • If you are paid irregularly, your actual monthly payslip can differ from a simple annual average.
